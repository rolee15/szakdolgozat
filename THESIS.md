# Thesis Topic

Most Japanese language learning applications available online have been designed as general language tools. Due to the specific characteristics of the Japanese language and writing system, these tools are not always effective for learning. Since these applications focus on general languages, they do not go deep into the language, making the lessons ineffective and unable to maintain sufficient interest, which causes users to lose motivation more easily. The goal of my thesis is to create an application in which lessons and exercises are designed with the peculiarities of the Japanese language in mind, and present the fascinating aspects of Japanese in the most engaging way possible.

In my thesis, I will create a web-based application for practicing reading and writing Japanese characters, understanding texts, and acquiring Japanese grammar rules as well. My goal is to provide a person learning Japanese with all the necessary support in one place for language acquisition at various difficulty levels. Furthermore, the lessons will be interesting and challenging in order to maintain interest and motivation. I will achieve this through various practice exercises, a structured learning path, and verification tests at the end of lessons and modules. I will implement the lessons and learning path in a customizable manner so that it can be easily modified later, providing an effective means for acquiring Japanese language and writing skills.
